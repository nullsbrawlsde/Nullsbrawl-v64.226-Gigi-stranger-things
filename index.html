New Mythic Brawler: Gigi

Gigi is the highlight addition in this update. She is a ballerina-themed assassin designed for fast movement, unexpected engagements, and quick repositioning.

Key Abilities:

  • A spinning melee attack that deals area damage and temporarily increases her mobility
  • A teleport Super that lets her appear instantly at a selected location
  • A return reposition option for escaping or re-engaging
  • A trait that charges her Super when she stays close to enemy projectiles or melee threats
  • Star Powers that enhance healing or increase Super charge rate
  • Gadgets that expand her charging zone or create temporary invisibility areas for her team

Gigi performs best when she picks the right moment to enter fights, uses cover wisely, and coordinates with teammates. She thrives in situations where she can pressure enemies, hit multiple targets, and escape before they can respond.

Upside Showdown: New Roguelite Game Mode

Upside Showdown transforms the classic Showdown experience into a stage-based roguelite challenge.

How It Works:

  1. Each run includes five stages
  2. You choose an upgrade before entering each stage
  3. Only the top four players progress to the next level
  4. Completing all stages gives the highest rewards

Every run feels different because of upgrade combinations and changing enemy behavior.
